Food

I think one of life's greatest pleasure is eating. I was born and raised in Philippines and we have amazing food. Here's some foods that I grew up eating:
Adobo: an authentic Filipino dish and is one of the mostly recognized Filipino foods. This dish is uniquely prepared by stewing meat of choice can be chicken or pork, in vinegar and soy sauce.

Sinigang: a sour soup native to the Philippines. This recipe uses pork as the main ingredient. Other proteins and seafood can also be used. Beef, shrimp, fish are commonly used to cook sinigang.
